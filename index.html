Character Profile

Stardust

Identity: The first Chinese VOCALOID4 songstress

Age: 16

Birthday: August 12

Height: 160cm

Voice provider: Chalili

Motif: Metatron’s Cube / Golden star

Stardust is the first Chinese VOCALOID4 songstress, and a girl character themed around the starry sky, the universe, singing, and geometric motifs. Her overall impression is clear, dreamy, quiet, and mysterious. She is a songstress born from ether.

Her key appearance features are long grayish-white hair, a soft pale blue or cobalt-blue glow, and golden eyes. Her hairstyle is especially important: four low ponytails, with the hair sections falling naturally downward and keeping a soft, weightless feeling. Please note that her hair color should not follow the official support color. Instead, please refer to the images and color codes in the later “Hair Color Reference” section.

Her outfit may refer to the main elements of her official costume, but it does not need to be drawn exactly the same. Possible reference elements include a deep blue or bluish-purple dress, yellow or gold inner lining, star-shaped decorations, black long gloves, and black over-the-knee socks or long boots. Triangular shapes, star motifs, and geometric line decorations may also be added to strengthen the starry, cosmic, and geometric impression.

Her main symbolic motif is Metatron’s Cube, which may be paired with golden star-shaped elements. It can appear in her hands, as a hair accessory, as part of the outfit pattern, or as a background decoration. The overall atmosphere should remain pure, calm, dreamlike, and slightly distant, like a quiet sea of stars. For casual outfits or original costume arrangements, the design does not need to strictly copy the official outfit as long as her key features and celestial feeling are preserved.

Key points for illustration:

Songstress, 16 years old, 160cm tall, four low ponytails, golden eyes, long grayish-white hair, pale blue or cobalt-blue glow, starry atmosphere, geometric motifs, golden stars, and Metatron’s Cube.
